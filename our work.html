Why us?

We at foot clinic plus off a very unique and different service. With 5 services under one roof you always feel like you are getting the best care from the best people. Foot Clinic Plus was established in 2005 and provides podiatry, foot surgery, biomechanics, chiropody and a physiotherapy service to the North East and beyond. They are a multidisciplinary team of registered health care professionals, all established and experienced in their chosen professions.
