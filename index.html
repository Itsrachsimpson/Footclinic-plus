We are a friendly team of professionals committed to bringing you the best medical care possible. Foot Clinic Plus was established in 2005 and provides podiatry, foot surgery, biomechanics, chiropody and a physiotherapy service to the North East and beyond. They are a multidisciplinary team of registered health care professionals, all established and experienced in their chosen professions.
